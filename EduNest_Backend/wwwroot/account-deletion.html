EduNest Account and Data Deletion Request

EduNest users can request deletion of their account and related personal data by contacting the support team by email.

How to submit a request

  1. Send an email to lndkhang278@gmail.com.
  2. Email subject: EduNest Account Deletion Request.
  3. In the email body, provide the email address or phone number used to register your EduNest account.

Data that will be deleted

EduNest will delete or anonymize account profile information, contact information, learning data, homework data, uploaded materials, and data related to student, parent, or tutor roles where permitted by law.

Data that may need to be retained

Some transaction, payment, support report, or compliance-related records may be retained for as long as necessary to meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, and maintain platform safety.

Account deletion requests are usually processed within 7 business days after EduNest verifies account ownership.
